## Findings

### 1. Voice-to-Text Field Documentation
- **Relevance: 8 | Novelty: 7**
- Source: HVAC/P Products (March 2026)
- AI voice-to-text tools are becoming a primary field documentation method. Techs dictate notes naturally on-site and AI converts them to structured reports. Addresses one of the biggest time sinks in field work.
- **Action taken:** Created new skill `operations/field-report-dictation.md` — transforms raw dictated notes into structured service reports with automatic HVAC term correction.

### 2. Predictive Maintenance & AFDD Going Mainstream
- **Relevance: 8 | Novelty: 7**
- Sources: OxMaint, Climate Experts AC, multiple industry analyses
- IoT sensor-driven predictive maintenance is shifting from pilot to operational standard. AFDD systems detect failure precursors 7–21 days before breakdown. Early adopters report 50% less downtime and 25–40% lower maintenance costs. Retrofit wireless sensors now install without electrical modification.
- **Action taken:** Created new skill `operations/predictive-maintenance-summary.md` — analyzes sensor data and maintenance history to produce prioritized maintenance reports with failure-risk scoring.

### 3. Interactive AI Field Troubleshooting
- **Relevance: 8 | Novelty: 6**
- Sources: HVAC/P Products, Arcticom Group, BTrained
- Techs are using AI as a live field assistant — asking it to list causes based on symptoms, suggest missed diagnostic steps, and interpret error codes. This is conversational and step-by-step, not just a pre-dispatch summary.
- **Action taken:** Enhanced `operations/service-call-diagnosis-brief.md` (v1.0 → v1.1) — added `interactive` mode for live, turn-by-turn diagnostic guidance in the field alongside the existing `brief` mode.
